Virtual Reality(VR) has recently become a huge advancement in the medical field. As we already know, VR has the capability to create almost realistic surroundings and environments, but it also has the capability to recreate the human body. This gives people in the medical field (including students) an inside look to possible situations without wasting valuable resources.

Companies such as Next Galaxy , have invested in making VR a more accessible tool for these purposes. Not only do they want it for medicinal reasons but also for education. Currently, they are working towards creating a CPR model to teach the proper way to help a choking victim.
